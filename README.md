** _**Bank Customer Churn Analysis****_

**Overview**

This project focuses on analyzing bank customer churn behavior using Python, SQL, and Power BI. The objective is to identify the key factors influencing customer retention and visualize business insights through an interactive dashboard.

****Key Business Insights****

Germany showed higher churn compared to other regions.
Middle-aged customers had higher churn rates.
Inactive customers were more likely to churn.
Customers using fewer products showed higher churn behavior.
Credit card ownership had less impact on churn.
